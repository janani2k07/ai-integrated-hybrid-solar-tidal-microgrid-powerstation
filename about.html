About Nauvahini

About Nauvahini (नौवाहिनी) ​Nauvahini represents a "Fleet of boats" or a "New model Boat for Water". We are a mission-driven initiative focused on "Innovation for Business Upliftment & Sustainability in Coastal & Hill Areas". Originally proposed for the MSME Idea Hackathon 5.0, our project serves as a transformative bridge between advanced technology and the traditional livelihoods of rural coastal India.

Our Core Innovation

We have developed an AI-Integrated Hybrid Solar-Tidal Floating Microgrid specifically designed for electrified maritime transport. By combining the power of the sun and the rhythm of the tides, we provide a continuous, 24/7 energy supply that does not depend on a single resource or an unreliable land-based grid.

Solar Power:

Captured via flexible or rigid photovoltaic panels optimized for marine environments. ​

Tidal Power:

Harnessed through submerged turbines that convert kinetic energy even during the night.

​AI Management:

Our systems use predictive analytics to forecast energy demand and optimize e-boat charging schedules.

​Floating Microgrid:

A modular, portable, and climate-resilient platform that resists rising sea levels and flooding.

Our Objectives & Impact:

​Nauvahini is dedicated to the economic and livelihood upliftment of rural fishermen and women.

​Economic Growth:

We help fishermen save significantly on diesel costs while providing power for cold storage and fish processing.

​Environmental Stewardship:

Our zero-carbon footprint model replaces polluting diesel engines with clean e-boats to preserve marine biodiversity.

​Community Empowerment:

Beyond transport, our microgrids provide electricity for essential services like local health centers and schools.

​Climate Resilience:

We promote renewable-based energy independence in vulnerable coastal zones through infrastructure built to withstand extreme weather.

Technical Roadmap

​Our project follows a structured 6-month development timeline:

​Planning & Feasibility:

Concept design and site confirmation.

​Design & Fabrication:

Engineering specifications and modular assembly.

​Installation & Commissioning:

Setting up turbines, panels, and integrated system testing.

​Operation:

Local training and data-managed tuning.
